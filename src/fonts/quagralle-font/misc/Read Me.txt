MARBELLYA CONDENSED SANS SERIF
-------------------------------------------------------------
Quagralle is a modern, versatile font based on hand lettered.
Supports OpenType, you can access everything in your glyphs
panel and make sure to turn on your ligatures or discretionary
ligatures and check out all stylistic alternates. Quagralle is
the great choice for watermark on photography, signature or
signature logo design, quotes, album cover, business card, and
many other design project.
